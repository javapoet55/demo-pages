Frequently asked questions

Do you really answer 24/7?
Yes. Our booking line is staffed every hour of every day, including nights, weekends, and holidays. You’ll talk to a real person and can schedule an appointment on the spot — never a voicemail box.
How fast can you come out?
Most repairs are handled same day. Book early and we’ll usually offer a window within hours. True emergencies — no heat, no cooling in extreme conditions, gas concerns — are dispatched right away.
What areas do you serve?
All of San Francisco, plus nearby cities like Daly City, South San Francisco, Brisbane, and Colma. If you’re not sure whether you’re in range, just call and we’ll confirm in seconds.
How does pricing work?
It’s flat-rate. We diagnose the issue and quote a fixed price before any work begins, so you approve the total up front — no hourly meter and no surprises on the final bill.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — we’re a licensed HVAC contractor (CSLB C-20) and fully insured. Our technicians are background-checked and EPA 608 certified for safe refrigerant handling.
What brands do you service?
All major residential brands, including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Bryant, Mitsubishi, and Daikin — for both heating and air conditioning systems.
