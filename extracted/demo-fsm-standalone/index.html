Questions

Good to know before you call.

Are you really available 24/7?
Yes. A live, licensed plumber answers our line every hour of every day — including weekends and holidays. You'll never hit a voicemail box or an offshore call center.
How fast can you get here in an emergency?
For true emergencies — burst pipes, active flooding, sewage backups — our typical arrival across San Francisco is under an hour. We'll always give you an honest ETA when you call rather than a vague window.
Do you charge flat-rate or by the hour?
Flat-rate. We diagnose on-site, then give you one fixed price to approve before any work begins. There's no hourly meter and no overtime surcharge — even in the middle of the night.
Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?
Yes — CSLB #1041792 (C-36 plumbing classification), fully bonded and insured. Every plumber is background-checked and arrives in a marked uniform.
What kinds of jobs do you handle?
Everything residential: emergency leaks and burst pipes, clogged and backed-up drains, sewer lines, water heaters (tank and tankless), toilets, faucets, fixtures, whole-home repipes, and slab-leak detection.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes for planned work like repipes, water-heater replacement, and fixture installs. Emergency and diagnostic visits carry a flat trip fee, which we credit toward the repair if you decide to proceed.
